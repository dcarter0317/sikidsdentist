Welcome to Staten Island Pediatric Dentistry

Welcome to Staten Island Pediatric Dentistry, the dental practice of Dr. Michelle Flanigan, Dr. Yelena Lange, and Dr. Sara Skurnick.

Dr. Michelle and Dr. Yelena are experienced board-certified pediatric dentists, and Dr. Sara Skurnick is a highly sought-after orthodontist in Staten Island. They are pleased to offer comprehensive pediatric dentistry for children of all ages and at all stages of dental development, from infants to young adults.

Our entire dental team is passionate about creating a supportive and nurturing environment for children. This is an experience that sets the stage for a lifetime of healthy, happy smiles.

Our Core Values

Our core values are the roadmap we follow to ensure that every interaction with you meets and exceeds your expectations for your child's care.

Compassion and empathy: Our approach to treating children is to provide the same care, compassion, and empathy we want for our own children.
Integrity and honesty: Our dentists believe in honesty and transparency and doing the right thing every time.
Exceptional treatments: Our dental team is committed to continued growth and providing your child with the latest and best that dentistry has to offer.

Comprehensive Pediatric Dentistry

At Staten Island Pediatric Dentistry, we provide the foundational dental care your child needs for excellent oral health, including dental exams, cleanings, and sealants. However, our pediatric dentists have training and skill that goes far beyond offering these basic services.

Dr. Michelle and Dr. Yelena are experienced board-certified pediatric dentists with experience in treating children in clinical and hospital settings. Their specialized training allows our pediatric dentists to provide your child with any treatment they need to maintain a healthy smile.

We offer emergency dentistry because kids are kids, and accidents are part of growing up. Please call us at the first sign of trouble, and our expert dental team will be happy to assist you.

Orthodontic Treatment for All Ages

Dr. Sara Skurnick is our well-respected orthodontist who has been treating children and adults in Staten Island and surrounding areas for over 30 years. If recommended, she provides early orthodontic treatment for children. Dr. Sara also provides traditional braces and Invisalign® clear braces for older teens and adults.

If you would like a consultation appointment with Dr. Sara, please call us at (718) 761-7316 to schedule.

Leading-Edge Laser Dentistry for Children

Much of what's involved with helping children have a great dental experience is providing comfortable, pain-free, and stress-free treatment. That is why we are so excited to introduce laser dentistry to our Staten Island pediatric dental office.

Laser dentistry offers numerous benefits, but the one parents find most appealing is the elimination of shots and drills in most cases. That's right! Using our Solea dental laser, your child's appointments are more efficient, meaning they spend less time in the dental chair. Learn more about the benefits of Solea laser dentistry.

Your Child's Comfort Is Our Priority

Everything we do is centered around your child's comfort and ensuring that every dental visit ends on a positive note. Some children cannot wait to hop into the dental chair, while others are much more hesitant.

That is okay! As pediatric dentists, Dr. Michelle and Dr. Yelena are trained to work with each unique child, including those with special needs.

As they get to know your child, our dentists learn the best ways to approach them, so that they feel safe and secure with us.

Hospital-Based Sedation and Dentistry

Many children benefit from sedation dentistry like nitrous oxide (sweet air or laughing gas). This supportive service helps the child feel calm. A calm child will have a more positive memory of the experience and allow our dentists to work most efficiently.

Some children have very special healthcare needs or extensive work that may best be addressed under general anesthesia. Staten Island Pediatric Dentistry has been providing hospital-based dentistry under the care of superb pediatric anesthesiologists since 1976.

We Look Forward to Welcoming Your Child

We are excited that you are considering Staten Island Pediatric Dentistry for your child's new dental home. Dr. Michelle, Dr. Yelena, and Dr. Sara would love to meet you and explain more about all we offer to maintain your child's healthy smile.

Our office hours are designed with our busy families in mind, and we are open six days a week, including Saturdays. We also have evening hours if your schedule does not allow you to come in during the day.

If your child has a dental emergency, please call us right away, and we will provide the guidance and the urgent treatment they need to protect their smile.
